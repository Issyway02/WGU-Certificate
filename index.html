Why Choose a WGU Certificate?

Gain practical skills you can use right away—whether you want to grow in your current role, become an expert at your company, or
pivot into something new. WGU Certificates are designed with input from over 1,000 companies, so what you learn reflects real-
world business needs.

Benefits of a Certificate

  • Job-Ready Skills: Curriculum developed with input from employers to reflect real workforce need

  • Never Alone: Dedicated mentors and career services resources guide you from start to finish University backed

  • Value Now, Value Later: Credits earned in a WGU Certificate can be applied to a WGU Degree later

  • University Backed: Earn real credentials from an accredited university with over 29 years of providing higher education
